MADAME JOURDAIN

What jargon is this?

MONSIEUR JOURDAIN

Non tener honta, questa star l'ultima affronta.

MADAME JOURDAIN

What in the world is all that?

MONSIEUR JOURDAIN

(Dancing and singing)

Hou la ba, Ba la chou, ba la ba, ba la da.

MADAME JOURDAIN

Alas! Oh Lord, my husband has gone mad.

MONSIEUR JOURDAIN

(Leaving)

Peace, insolent woman! Show respect to the Monsieur Mamamouchi.

MADAME JOURDAIN

Has he lost his mind? I must hurry to stop him from going out. Ah! Ah! This is the last straw! I see nothing but shame on all sides.

(She leaves)
